Welcome to Chronohymn

Chronohymn is a bardic music project weaving ritual sound, ancestral lore, and divine invocation into song. These are hymns for the gods, chants for solstice, and rhythms for those who remember. From the bones of the past to the fire of the present, our songs rise to echo across the wheel of the year.

Divine Harmony

A sacred anthem honoring the shared truths of pantheons, this track weaves unity and resonance into every beat.

Týr’s Oath

A war hymn of protection, justice, and honor—calling defenders to rise against cruelty and stand in sacred strength.

Solfire

A pounding ritual blaze for Summer Solstice. Tribal drums, burning rhythm—this is fire you can feel in your chest.
