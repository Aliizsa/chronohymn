Welcome to Chronohymn

Chronohymn is a bardic music project weaving ritual sound, ancestral lore, and divine invocation into song. These are hymns for the gods, chants for solstice, and rhythms for those who remember. From the bones of the past to the fire of the present, our songs rise to echo across the wheel of the year.
